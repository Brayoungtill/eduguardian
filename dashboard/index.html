ℹ️ About EduGuardian

EduGuardian monitors Ghana Education Service (GES) school tablets for unauthorized SIM card usage. When a student inserts a SIM card, the system logs the time, SIM details, and all app activity during that session and uploads it to this dashboard for school administrators to review.

Version 1.0.0 · Built for Ghana Education Service
